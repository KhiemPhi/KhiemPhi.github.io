I am an Honors Computer Science Major at Stony Brook University where I am actively involved in undergraduate research at the Human Language Analysis Beings Lab (HLAB), the Data Management and Biomedical Data Analytics Lab (BMI-DB) and the Computer Vision Lab (CV Lab). My research interests includes Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Human-Computer Interaction and Computing for Social Good.

I am a Senior Honors Computer Science Major at Stony Brook University. I have conducted research under the supervision of Professor Minh Hoai Nguyen, Professor H.Andrew Schwartz, Professor Ritiwik Banerjee and Professor Fusheng Wang.

I am currently working on my Senior Honors Project, "Quantifying and Detecting Whataboutism in Social Media Comments". I am also involved with the EyeCanDo Project.
